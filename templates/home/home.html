Do I need a cylinder to make online order from QwikGas?

No. You do not need to have a cylinder to order any of your desired QwikGas packages. QwikGas provides you with cost-free, clean, safe, and premium quality cylinder in excellent condition. Hence, you can seamlessly enjoy cooking with gas with no worries.

How many cylinders can I order at a time?

QwikGas allows you to order cooking gas up to ten (10) 12.5 kg cylinders or its equivalence at a time, after KYC (Know Your Customer) has been completed.

How can I make payment for my QwikGas order?

You can pay using your credit/debit cards (including Masters card, Visa card, Verve card), USSD and bank transfer through the QwikGas checkout and partner payment gateway.
