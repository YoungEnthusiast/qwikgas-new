#1 B2B Cookstove-as-a-Service brand.

Energy poverty is prevalent in emerging markets where over 2.8 billion people still cook with dirty fuels. QwikGas® is solving energy poverty among the underserved in emerging markets via our pioneering B2B cookstove-as-a-service (CsaaS) using technology. Our qwikgas.ai WebApp fulfils on-demand clean energy fuel ordering. We utilized AI technology for the common goods for anticipatory energy delivery to businesses and track customers credit worthiness rating. QwikGas® is a cleantech brand with embedded finance and an AI-first operations.

Our embedded finance play allows us to support businesses with flexible payment options for clean energy utilization, which include pay small small (instalment payment) and paylater (buy, use, make money, and then pay), as low earnings deter many businesses from using liquefied petroleum gas (LPG). Hence, we make money by ensuring our clients are empowered to make money.

With our AI-first approach, QwikGas® is positioned to be a data-driven cleantech company. QwikGas® allows businesses to track their clean energy use and monitor their expenses on clean fuel. End-users clean cooking data are generally scarce, QwikGas® seeks to make this a thing of the past.

QwikGas® pioneering cookstove-as-a-service (CsaaS) makes it easy for businsses to cook with clean fuel with no need for prior investment in cookstove hardware and accessories. With our cookstove-as-a-service (CsaaS), QwikGas® removes the initial cost of owning a cylinder, which is identified as major reason for non-adoption of LPG. QwikGas® as a brand equally help solve challenges of incessant fire outbreak at retail due to decanting and other illegal and unsafe practices. Our customers are given access to quality and clean LPG cylinders at no added cost.

QwikGas® is the go-to brand for owners and operators of canteens, restaurants, eateries, bakeries, caterers, shawarma and Mai tea joints, home parties and events, ‘Owambes’, boarding schools and other large cooking needs.
